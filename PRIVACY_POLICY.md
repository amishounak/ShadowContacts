# Privacy Policy for Shadow Contacts

**Last updated: March 28, 2026**

## Overview

Shadow Contacts ("the app") is a private, offline contact management app for Android. Your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and what we never do with it.

## Data Collection

**We collect no data.** Shadow Contacts does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any personal information or usage data.

## Data Storage

All contact data you enter or import into Shadow Contacts is stored exclusively on your device using Android's SQLite database (via Room). This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never uploaded to any server
- Is never synced to Google, iCloud, or any cloud service
- Is not accessible to the developer or any third party

## Permissions

### READ_CONTACTS
Used solely to allow you to import contacts from your phone's address book into Shadow Contacts. We read your contacts only at the moment you choose to import — we do not store, transmit, or access your phone contacts at any other time.

## Third-Party Services

Shadow Contacts does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

## Children's Privacy

Shadow Contacts does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features directed at children.
